Our History

Nigeria's Creus, born from founder Ehijie Omijie's passion for craftsmanship, is a beacon of tradition. Each garment, a masterpiece of elegance, tells stories of bespoke artistry. From its humble tailor shop beginnings, Creus has blossomed into a symbol of luxury, attracting patrons seeking more than clothing. It's a sanctuary where fabric meets creativity, stitching tales of heritage. With every stitch, Creus honors its legacy, blending tradition with innovation. Today, it stands as a testament to timeless elegance, offering an experience beyond mere attire. Welcome to Creus, where dreams are tailored into reality with a touch of Nigerian flair.
